Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our pricing and billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard) and bank transfers. Enterprise customers can also arrange invoicing terms.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a trial for WhastsApp platforms. This gives you full access to test all features before purchasing the token.

What happens if I exceed my contact limit?

For China Platform, you'll receive a notification when approaching your limit. You can upgrade to a higher tier or purchase additional contact capacity. For WhatsApp, there are no contact limits.
