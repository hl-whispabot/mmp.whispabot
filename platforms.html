Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our platforms

Can I use both platforms simultaneously?

Yes! Our platforms are completely independent. Many businesses use the China platform to capture new leads and the WhatsApp platform to engage their existing customer base globally.

What's the difference in pricing models?

The China platform uses a subscription model with automated lead capture function. The WhatsApp platform is pay-as-you-use, starting from $0.0025 per message, with no subscription fee.

How quickly can I get started?

WhatsApp platform setup takes 24-48 hours. China platform setup requires 3-5 business days due to XHS verification requirements.

Do I need existing customers to use these platforms?

The China platform is designed to help you acquire new customers from XHS. The WhatsApp platform works best when you already have customer contact data to engage.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer free trial for WhatsApp platform to help you test the features. Drop us a message!
