Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WhatsApp Business messaging

What's the difference between WhatsApp Business and WhatsApp Business API?

WhatsApp Business app is for small businesses with one device. WhatsApp Business API (what we provide) is for medium-large businesses, allowing multiple team members, automation, integrations, and higher message volumes.

How do I get a WhatsApp Business number?

We help you set up your WhatsApp Business number. You need a phone number dedicated to WhatsApp (can't be used with regular WhatsApp), business verification documents, and Facebook Business Manager account.

What are opt-in messages vs template messages?

Opt-in messages are free and can be sent within 24 hours of a customer messaging you. Template messages are pre-approved messages you can send anytime (charged per message) for notifications, marketing and re-engagement.

Can I send promotional messages on WhatsApp?

Yes, but only to customers who have opted in and using pre-approved marketing templates. You cannot send unsolicited promotional messages.

How long does it take to get started?

Setup typically takes 3-5 business days including business verification, number approval and platform configuration. Once approved, you can start messaging immediately.

Can I use my existing WhatsApp number?

No, you need a new dedicated phone number for WhatsApp Business API. The number cannot be registered with regular WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app.

Is there a message volume limit?

Meta assigns messaging limits based on your phone number quality rating and business verification tier. Start at 1,000 conversations/day and can scale to unlimited with good practices. We help you scale responsibly.
