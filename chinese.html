Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the China Lead Capture Platform

How does the platform capture leads from XHS?

The platform integrates with XHS via API. When users drop a lead in XHS, our system automatically captures the information, sends welcome messages and tags them based on the source post.

Do I need a WeCom Official Account?

Yes, you need a verified WeCom Official Account to be able to communicate with user's WeChat. If you don't have one, we can help you through the setup and verification process, which typically takes 3-5 business days.

Can I customize the automated messages?

Absolutely! You have full control over message templates. Create different messages for different XHS posts, personalize with user data, include images/videos, and set up multi-step follow-up sequences.

Is my customer data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption, comply with GDPR and Chinese data regulations, and never share your data with third parties. You own 100% of your customer data and can export it anytime.

Can I integrate with my existing CRM?

Yes! We offer native integrations with major CRM platforms and a REST API for custom integrations. Leads can be automatically synced to your CRM in real-time with all metadata and tags.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide dedicated customer success managers, business hour technical support, onboarding training, best practices consultation, and regular strategy reviews to ensure you maximize your ROI.
